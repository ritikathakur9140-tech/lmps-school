About Our School

Welcome to Lucknow Model Public School, Awagojha, Hasanganj, Unnao. Our institution is dedicated to creating confident and responsible citizens through a beautifully balanced student-centered learning approach.

We emphasize strong moral values, advanced teaching methods, and extensive co-curricular activities to make learning truly engaging and future-ready for our pupils.

Our World-Class Facilities

Smart Classrooms

Interactive and audio-visual powered learning spaces designed to make tough educational concepts extremely easy and visual for kids.

Modern Computer Lab

Equipped with modern computer setups to teach digital literacy and fundamental computer skills right from the early years.

Safe & Nurturing Campus

A completely healthy, secure, and disciplined infrastructure where children learn through play and creative challenges safely.

Activity-Based Learning
